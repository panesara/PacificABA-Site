Career Opportunities

Pacific ABA Academy is growing and looking for passionate professionals to join our team. We're seeking qualified individuals who are committed to making a difference in the lives of children with autism and related disabilities.

ABA Interventionist

Location: Surrey, BC

Description: We are looking for dedicated ABA interventionists to deliver evidence-based treatment to children in our programs. Candidates should have training in ABA principles and a passion for working with children.

Qualifications:

  • Knowledge of ABA principles and techniques
  • Experience working with children with autism or developmental disabilities
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to work collaboratively as part of a team

BCBA Supervisor

Location: Surrey, BC

Description: We are seeking a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) to provide clinical supervision and program oversight. This is an excellent opportunity for an experienced BCBA to lead and mentor a growing team.

Qualifications:

  • Current BCBA certification
  • Experience supervising ABA programs
  • Strong leadership and clinical skills
  • Knowledge of current ABA best practices

Administrative Support

Location: Surrey, BC

Description: We are looking for organized and detail-oriented individuals to support our administrative operations. This role involves scheduling, client communication, and general office management.

Qualifications:

  • Strong organizational and communication skills
  • Proficiency with office software and systems
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and priorities
  • Professional demeanor and customer service orientation

Why Work With Us?

  • Work with a dedicated team committed to excellence
  • Opportunity to make a real difference in children's lives
  • Professional development and training opportunities
  • Collaborative and supportive work environment
  • Competitive compensation

How to Apply

Interested in joining our team? Please send your resume and cover letter to careers@pacificaba.com or call us at 778-565-1064 to discuss opportunities.

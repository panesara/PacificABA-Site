Welcome to Pacific ABA Academy

Pacific ABA Academy is dedicated to providing scientifically validated ABA treatment to individuals diagnosed with Autism and related disabilities. It is our goal to provide motivation and self-empowerment to teach individuals touched with special needs that the sky is the limit.

Care

On-Site Group Program

We house an intensive group ABA program for children ages 2-6 years old for 16-20 hours a week.

The on-site group ABA program encompasses a "pre-school" type setting where children attend a highly structured group ABA Program for 4 hours a day. This program involves two critical components: One-to-one intensive teaching sessions following the Lovaas method and other scientifically validated approaches, and natural environment teaching sessions to generalize the skills taught in the intensive teaching sessions.

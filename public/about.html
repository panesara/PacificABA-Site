About Pacific ABA Academy

Our Mission

Pacific ABA Academy is dedicated to providing scientifically validated ABA treatment to individuals diagnosed with Autism and related disabilities. Our mission is to provide motivation and self-empowerment to teach individuals with special needs that the sky is the limit.

Our Leadership

Our academy is led by Dr. Ramen Saggu, PhD, BCBA, who brings extensive experience and expertise in Applied Behavior Analysis. Under Dr. Saggu's clinical supervision, our team delivers evidence-based, individualized services for children and families in British Columbia.

Our Team

Pacific ABA Academy employs qualified interventionists who are trained in evidence-based ABA practices. Our team is committed to providing compassionate, professional care and ongoing support to families throughout their journey with us.

Why Choose Us?

  • Evidence-based ABA treatment following the Lovaas method and other scientifically validated approaches
  • Clinical supervision by Dr. Ramen Saggu, PhD, BCBA
  • Individualized programs tailored to each child's unique needs
  • Experienced, qualified interventionists
  • Comprehensive assessments and progress monitoring
  • Family-centered approach to treatment
  • Convenient location in Surrey, BC

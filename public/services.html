Our Services

On-Site Group Program

We house an intensive group ABA program for children ages 2-6 years old for 16-20 hours a week. The on-site group ABA program encompasses a "pre-school" type setting where children attend a highly structured group ABA Program for 4 hours a day.

This program involves two critical components:

  • One-to-one intensive teaching sessions following the Lovaas method and other scientifically validated approaches
  • Natural environment teaching sessions to generalize the skills taught in the intensive teaching sessions

Four qualified interventionists work across six children, with 1-to-1 instruction provided during the intensive teaching sessions. Group activities include: art time, snack time, community outings, active games, and board games.

Private Assessments

Pacific ABA Academy is now offering Private Assessments for Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). Our comprehensive assessments are designed to identify strengths and areas for development.

Pre-Diagnostic Intervention Program

Early intervention is crucial for children with developmental delays. Our Pre-Diagnostic Intervention Program provides support for children who may be at risk or showing early signs of developmental differences.

Speech & Language Therapy

Integrated communication support by Speech-Language Pathologists. Our SLPs work as part of our interdisciplinary autism team, using evidence-based, autism-affirming practices to develop meaningful communication skills. Services include family coaching and strategies for carryover at home and school.
